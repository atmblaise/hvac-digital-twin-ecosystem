# Thermodynamics Engine

## Overview
This module models fundamental energy balances used in HVAC and industrial thermal systems.

It forms the core of the digital twin by describing how energy is transferred in steady-state systems.

## Key Concepts
- First Law of Thermodynamics
- Steady-flow energy systems
- Enthalpy-based energy balance

## Core Equation
 $ Q = m_dot (h_out - h_in) $

## Applications
- Boilers
- Heat exchangers
- Refrigeration systems
- HVAC energy analysis
